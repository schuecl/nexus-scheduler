The database migration Job ({{ include "nexus-scheduler.fullname" . }}-migrate-{{ .Release.Revision }})
runs `prisma db push` and retries for up to 5 minutes if Postgres isn't
reachable yet — normal on a fresh install, since it starts at the same
time as the bundled PostgreSQL/Redis instances rather than waiting on
them. api/worker pods may show a few CrashLoopBackOff restarts until the
migration Job succeeds; that's expected self-healing, not a separate
problem. Check `kubectl logs job/{{ include "nexus-scheduler.fullname" . }}-migrate-{{ .Release.Revision }}`
if api/worker are still failing well after that Job completes.

SMTP (§5) is not a K8s Secret — configure it from the Admin UI after
first login; it's stored (password encrypted) in the app's own database.
